The Pokemon League is a series of challenges, culminating in the Elite 4 Challenge. Are you up to the test?

What exactly is the Pokemon League?

The Pokemon League is a competitive challenge held in many regions all over the world. Most regions follow a series of 8 gyms, followed up by the Elite 4 challenge for those who have succesfully defeated everything preceding it.

What is the Badge System?

Badges are a way of tracking how many gyms a trainer has successfully defeated! Before attempting the Elite 4, your amount of badges must be verified at the entrance.
